# Change Control (Policies & Procedures)

## Purpose
Ensure policies are consistent, approved, and auditable.

## What is controlled
- Policies (GOVERNANCE/)
- Operational procedures (OPERATIONS/)
- Scholarship/billing compliance docs (NCSEAA-K12-PROGRAMS/, SCHOLARSHIPS-NC/)
- Family-facing handbooks (FAMILIES/)

## Change workflow
1) Propose change in a PR (pull request) or commit
2) Include:
   - Summary of change
   - Reason (risk, compliance, improvement)
   - Effective date
   - What documents are impacted
3) Review by:
   - Policy Owner (required)
   - Safety/Compliance Lead (required for child safety, discipline, privacy)
   - Finance Lead (required for billing/refunds/scholarships)
4) Approve & merge
5) Update:
   - CHANGELOG section inside the doc
   - `ACCREDITATION/COMPLIANCE-BINDER-INDEX.md` entry

## Emergency changes
If urgent (safety/legal):
- Implement immediately
- Mark as “Emergency”
- Complete full review within 5 business days

## Versioning rules
- Use dates: “Effective: YYYY-MM-DD”
- Keep prior versions in git history; do not overwrite without explanation
