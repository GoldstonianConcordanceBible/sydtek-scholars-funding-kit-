# Staff / Contractor Code of Conduct

## Expectations
- Treat students and families with respect
- Maintain professional boundaries
- Follow lane separation and communication rules
- Protect privacy and confidential information
- Follow all safety and reporting procedures

## Prohibited
- Harassment, bullying, discrimination
- Sharing sensitive student information outside approved processes
- Unapproved private communication channels with students
- Any retaliation against a reporter of concerns

## Acknowledgment
All staff/contractors must sign an acknowledgment (stored privately).
