# Child Protection Policy (Hybrid)

Effective: [TBD-YYYY-MM-DD]
Applies to: all staff, contractors, volunteers, tutors

## Purpose
Ensure student safety through clear boundaries, supervision, and reporting processes.

## Core principles
- Safety first
- Two-lane clarity (Lane 1 support vs Lane 2 enrollment)
- Appropriate communication
- Prompt escalation of concerns

## Boundaries & conduct
- No private 1:1 communications with students outside approved channels/policies
- No sharing personal contact info with students unless explicitly authorized by School policy
- No meetings without appropriate oversight per program rules
- Maintain professionalism in all communications

## Supervision (online/virtual)
- Use approved platforms only
- Record sessions when appropriate and lawful (policy-driven)
- Parent/guardian visibility for Lane 1 sessions unless otherwise documented

## Screening & training
- Background screening requirements per role (stored privately)
- Mandatory annual training: safety, reporting, privacy, online conduct

## Enforcement
Violations result in corrective action up to termination/removal from program.
