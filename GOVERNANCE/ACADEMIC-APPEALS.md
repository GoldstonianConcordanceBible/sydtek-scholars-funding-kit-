# Academic Appeals (Lane 2)

Effective: [TBD-YYYY-MM-DD]

## Scope
Applies to Lane 2 (Private School Enrollment) academic decisions:
- grades, promotion, placement, credit decisions

## Process
1) Parent requests review within 10 business days of decision
2) Records Lead reviews evidence (assessments, work samples)
3) Decision issued within 10 business days
4) Final escalation to Program Director within 5 business days

## Recordkeeping
Appeal files are RESTRICTED and stored privately.
