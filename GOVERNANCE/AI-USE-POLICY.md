# AI Use Policy (Hybrid)

## Purpose
Use AI responsibly while protecting student privacy.

## Allowed uses (examples)
- lesson planning assistance (no student PII)
- generating practice problems
- drafting parent communications (no student PII)
- teacher professional support

## Not allowed
- uploading student PII or restricted records into AI tools without explicit approved secure process
- using AI as the sole decision-maker for grades/placement/discipline

## Human review
All AI-assisted outputs used for instruction or decisions require human review.
