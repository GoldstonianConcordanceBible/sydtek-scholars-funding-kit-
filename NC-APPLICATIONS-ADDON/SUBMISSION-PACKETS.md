# Submission Packets — Flowchart C (Hybrid) ONLY

## Packet Lane 1 — Homeschool Support (DNPE Homeschool)
Use when: families operate a DNPE homeschool and purchase support services.

Keep ready:
- Lane 1 attendance record template (family-maintained; we provide template)
- Lane 1 annual standardized testing policy template (family-maintained; we provide template)
- Lane 1 immunization compliance policy template (family-maintained; we provide template)
- Lane 1 inspection prep guidance (family-facing)

Publishable:
- templates and guides
Private:
- any family/student personal info (do not publish)

------------------------------------------------------------

## Packet Lane 2 — Private School Enrollment (DNPE Private School)
Use when: student is officially enrolled in our private school program.

Keep ready:
- Student records policy (release + retention)
- Attendance policy + logs
- Immunization compliance policy + storage
- Staff/contractor roles and safeguarding policy
- Billing policy separating required vs optional fees

Publishable:
- policies (sanitized)
Private:
- student records, immunization records, staff background check docs

------------------------------------------------------------

## Packet Lane 2-Optional — NCSEAA K12 Programs Participation
Use when: Lane 2 intends to accept scholarship-funded tuition/fees.

Keep ready:
- New school registration checklist
- Signed nonpublic school agreement (version controlled)
- Tuition & Fee Schedule (required vs optional separated)
- Allowable vs Not Allowable costs reference
- Withdrawal/refund policy
- No parent reimbursement policy
- Background check attestation (highest authority role)
- Admin calendar for annual tasks (tuition/fee schedule deadlines)

Publishable:
- tuition/fee schedule (family-facing)
- withdrawal/refund policy
Private:
- banking, W-9, vendor forms, background check docs, student roster details
