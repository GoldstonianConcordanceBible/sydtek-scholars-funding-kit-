# School Admin Operations Calendar (NCSEAA K12 Programs) — Draft

## January
- Monitor MyPortal Taskpad for Tuition & Fee Schedule task (often begins late January)
- Prepare next year’s tuition/fee schedule draft

## February
- New school registration window (per NCSEAA notice)
- Submit required onboarding documents

## June
- New schools: tuition/fee schedule due by June 30 (if in registration process)

## July
- Returning schools: tuition/fee schedule due July 15
- Confirm billing categories and publish family-facing tuition/fees
