## Allowable (examples)
- Required tuition
- Fees required of all students in a category (e.g., technology, books, curriculum)

## Not allowable (examples)
- Lunch
- Optional fees (field trips, sports, extracurriculars)
- Non-academic fees
- After-school care
- Capital/building fees

School controls:
- We separate required academic fees from optional/non-allowable fees in invoicing and statements.
