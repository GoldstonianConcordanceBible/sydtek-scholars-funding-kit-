# Track Decision Matrix — Flowchart C (Hybrid) ONLY

This project is officially **Hybrid**:
- Lane 1: DNPE Homeschool Support
- Lane 2: DNPE Private School Enrollment
- Optional: NCSEAA K12 Programs for Lane 2 scholarship payments

| Lane | What it is | Who controls NOI | Records kept by us | Can accept NCSEAA scholarship payments |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Lane 1 | Homeschool support services | Family | Limited service records only | No |
| Lane 2 | Private school enrollment | School | Full student recordkeeping | Yes (optional, if approved/participating) |

## Messaging rules
- Lane 1 marketing: “support, tutoring, curriculum coaching”
- Lane 2 marketing: “enrollment, tuition, official records”
- Scholarships: only mentioned under Lane 2 and only if actually participating
