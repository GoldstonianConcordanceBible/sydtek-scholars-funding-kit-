# Billing Codebook (Hybrid Lane Separation)

## Purpose
Prevent billing mistakes and keep scholarship compliance clean (Lane 2 only).

## Lane 1 (Homeschool Support) — SERVICE CODES
These are service fees. Not scholarship-funded tuition.
- L1-COACH: Parent coaching
- L1-TUTOR: Tutoring sessions
- L1-PLAN: Curriculum planning package
- L1-ASSESS: Learning assessment (service)
- L1-MEM: Membership/support subscription

## Lane 2 (Private School Enrollment) — SCHOOL CODES
### Required Tuition (allowable for scholarships where applicable)
- L2-TUIT: Tuition

### Required Academic Fees (allowable only if required of all in category)
- L2-TECH: Technology fee (required)
- L2-BOOK: Books/curriculum fee (required)
- L2-TEST: Testing fee (required)
- L2-ENRL: Enrollment fee (required if required of all)

### Optional / Non-Allowable (do not bundle with required fees)
- L2-OPT-CLUB: Clubs
- L2-OPT-TRIP: Field trips
- L2-OPT-SPORT: Sports
- L2-OPT-AFTER: Aftercare

## Invoice Rules
1) Never mix Lane 1 and Lane 2 charges on the same invoice line items without clear separation.
2) Never label Lane 1 as “tuition.”
3) Optional fees must be clearly marked as optional and separated.
