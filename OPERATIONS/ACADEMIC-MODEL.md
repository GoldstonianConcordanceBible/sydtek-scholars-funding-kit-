# Academic Model (Hybrid Overview)

## Lane 1 (Homeschool Support)
- We provide guidance, coaching, tutoring, and planning templates
- Families choose curriculum and maintain homeschool compliance records
- Progress tracking is service-based (not official school records)

## Lane 2 (Private School Enrollment)
- School maintains attendance and academic records
- Instruction model includes:
  - daily literacy + math expectations
  - scheduled assessments/benchmarks
  - intervention supports where needed

## Instructional cadence (example)
- Weekly: instruction + check-ins
- Monthly: progress review
- Quarterly: benchmark assessment and parent conference
